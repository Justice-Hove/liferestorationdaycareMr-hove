Investing in Our Children, Restoring Our Community

Life Restoration Day Care stands as a pillar of hope and excellence in early childhood education. As a dedicated English-medium NPO, we provide more than just supervision; we offer a foundation of linguistic proficiency and academic readiness. Our mission is to bridge the educational gap, ensuring that every child in our care is equipped with the tools to succeed in a globalized world. Through professional care and community partnership, we are restoring the future, one child at a time.

Why Choose Us?

Unlocking Potential through Excellence.

Choosing a daycare is the first major step in your child's academic journey. We offer a curriculum that balances structured English learning with creative play. Our small class sizes ensure personalized attention, while our status as a registered NPO guarantees that our priority is always the progress of the child, not profit.

A Warm Welcome to Our Community

We believe that "it takes a village to raise a child." Life Restoration Day Care is an open door for all community members. Whether you are a parent seeking a safe haven or a neighbor looking to support our mission, we welcome you with open arms. Together, we can create a nurturing environment that uplifts every family.

A Holistic Approach to Development

At Life Restoration Day Care, we understand that the early years are the most critical for brain development and social integration. Our English-medium curriculum is specifically designed to transition children into primary school with confidence and a high level of communication skill. By immersing students in a language-rich environment early on, we remove the barriers that often hinder academic success later in life.

Furthermore, our after-school support program ensures that the learning doesn't stop when the school bell rings. We provide a supervised space for homework assistance, ensuring that students remain on track with their schooling while their parents are at work. This dual focus on Early Childhood Development and ongoing student support makes us a unique resource for restoration and growth within the community.
